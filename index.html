Welcome

How Basement Theater Works

This app is your family movie shelf. Pick a profile, browse the library, and open titles straight from Google Drive.

Library

Browse by category or A-Z. Titles with covers rise to the top, and the featured pick always uses a covered title.

Profiles

Each profile keeps its own favorites, watchlist, continue watching, and age filter.

Favorites

Tap the star to move a title into the Favorites tab. Remove it there and it returns to the main library.

Play

Click a poster for more details, then use Play or Preview to open the Drive link.

Surprise Me

Use Shuffle for a random pick from everything, the current category, or kid-safe titles only.

Trash Review

That tab is just a holding area for broken-link review. It does not affect the main library unless you choose to clean things up.
